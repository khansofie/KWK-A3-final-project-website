The Problem

The divide in STEM exposure between underprivileged and affluent communities contribute to a knowledge disparity due to differences in ethnic backgrounds and socioeconomic status. Solutions emerge from diverse backgrounds and perspectives, making it vital to address the lack of representation and exposure to innovation inclusive for all.

Data & Statistics

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/komalkhetlani/out-of-school-rates-global-data

The data provides information of the percentages of Total % of children who were out of schools. The regions more affected by this issue are Sub-Saharan Africa, South Asia, and Middle East and North Africa.

https://data.world/city-of-ny/svyi-maaj/workspace/file?filename=minority-and-women-owned-business-enterprise-statistics-1.csv

This study demonstartes how many tech companies have more female employees over male employees.They also lack people of color compared to white employees.

With the lack of diversity within tech industries, it is apparent that the indutry may have biases when creating products due to the creators. Thus, we need a variety of people to create innovative and unbiased designs.

https://data.world/finance/women-fortune-500-ceos

Studies show that the amount of women fortune 500 CEOs throughout the years have increased, but only by merre single digits.

With the lack of women CEOs there continues to be a lack of women who are leaders. This causes a lack of representation for young girls and discourages them from being innovators.
